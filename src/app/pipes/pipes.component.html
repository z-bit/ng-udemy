Pipes

Built-in Pipes
  • uppercase - {{myValue}}   >>>   {{myValue | uppercase}}
  • slice:3:7 - {{myValue}}   >>>   {{myValue | slice:3:7}}
  • slice:3:7 | uppercase - {{myValue}}   >>>   {{myValue | slice:3:7 | uppercase}}
  • date - {{myDate}}   >>>   {{myDate | date }}
  • date:'dd.MM.yy' - {{myDate}}   >>>   {{myDate | date:'dd.MM.yy' }}
  • Pipes in angular.io
Custom Pipes
  • ng g p double (ng generate pipe double)
  • double - {{myPrice}}   >>>   {{myPrice | double }}
  • brutto:{{UstInProzent}} - {{myPrice}}   >>>   {{myPrice | brutto:UstInProzent }}

Async Pipe
  • It takes a Promise/Observable and makes the values available after they arrived.
  • no pipe, just {{ asyncValue }}: {{asyncValue}}
  • async pipe {{ asyncValue | async }}: {{asyncValue | async}}
